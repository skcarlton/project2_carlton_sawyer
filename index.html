About Me

Hi! I'm Sawyer!

In August, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in public relations from the University of Florida where I simultaneously earned a certificate in graphic design. I am now working on completing my Master's from the University of Florida in Mass Communications with a specialization in Web Design and Online Communications. While my focus is mainly on digital design, I also have an interest in campaign development and team collaboration. This past year I was fortunate enough to put this all into practice as a member of the UFPRSSA Bateman Team working with the United States Census Bureau to promote the 2020 Census. In May, our team was honored to be selected as the first runner-up out of 57 entries from across the country.

In the summer of 2019, I worked in Melbourne, Australia as the Creative Services Intern for MK Media, a digital and public relations agency. Here I gained a global perspective of brand building and learned how the communications industry operates in other countries around the world.
